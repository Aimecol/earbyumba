Request an Appointment

Meeting Guidelines

Advance Notice

Please submit your request at least 3-5 business days in advance.

Meeting Duration

Standard meetings are scheduled for 30 minutes. Longer sessions may be arranged if needed.

Confirmation

You will receive confirmation within 24-48 hours via email or phone.

Emergency Situations

For urgent pastoral care, please call the diocese office directly at +250 788 123 456.

Cancellations

Please notify us at least 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel or reschedule.

Accompaniment

You may bring a family member or friend if needed. Please mention this in your request.
